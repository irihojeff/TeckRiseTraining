About AUCA

The Adventist University of Central Africa (AUCA), known as MUDENDE, is a Seventh-day Adventist institution of higher learning that operates on the basis of the worldview: "God is our Creator and Sustainer of the Universe and the source of true knowledge."

Description of IT Faculty

The Faculty of Information Technology is a unique part of the School of Sciences at AUCA, composed of several engineering and science departments.

The programs are designed to provide hands-on skills training with a focus on design, construction, implementation, and maintenance of both hardware and software components of modern systems.
